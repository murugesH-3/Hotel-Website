The Pangat Experience

In the heart of Belagavi, we preserve a dying art. Our kitchen is built around the Chulha—a traditional earthen stove fueled by wood and charcoal.

"It is the slow smoke, the gentle lick of the flame against the copper vessels, and the soul of the hearth that infuses our food with a flavor no modern gas burner can replicate."
